# Polynomial-Time Algorithms for Prime Factorization and Discrete Logarithms on a Quantum Computer
**Venue / Year**: SIAM Journal on Computing 1997（FOCS 1994 preliminary; arXiv quant-ph/9508027）
**Authors**: Peter W. Shor
**Read on**: 2026-05-14 (in lesson 3.11)
**Status**: full PDF (`assets/papers/shor-1994.pdf`)
**One-line**: 證明量子電腦能在 polynomial 時間 factor integers 與 solve discrete logarithm；徹底破壞所有經典公鑰密碼學 (RSA/DH/ECC) 的 long-term 安全性；觸發 NIST PQ standardization 與整個 post-quantum cryptography 領域。

## Problem
1994 年所有部署的公鑰密碼學基於：
- **Integer Factorization Problem (FACT)**: 給 N = pq, find p, q. 最佳 classical: GNFS, sub-exponential O(exp(c · n^(1/3) · log^(2/3) n))。
- **Discrete Logarithm Problem (DLP)**: 給 g^x mod p, find x. 最佳 classical: index calculus / GNFS, sub-exponential。

Shor 問: quantum computer 能不能 polynomial-time 解這些問題？

## Contribution
1. **Quantum factoring algorithm**: 用 quantum Fourier transform (QFT) 解 「period finding」problem，將 factoring reduce 到 period finding。Polynomial time in log N。
2. **Quantum DLP algorithm**: 類似 framework，period finding 解 multiplicative group 的 hidden subgroup。
3. **Killing all of**:
   - RSA (FACT-based)。
   - DSA (DLP-based)。
   - Diffie-Hellman (DLP-based)。
   - ECDSA / Ed25519 / ECDH (ECDLP-based, also period finding)。
   - Pairing-based crypto (BLS)。
4. **Quantum resource estimate** (later refined by Gidney-Ekerå 2021): factor RSA-2048 需 ~20 million physical qubits + ~8 hours wall time。

## Method (high-level)
**Quantum Factoring (Shor's algorithm)**:
```text
Input: N (composite)

1. Choose random a in [2, N-1]; if gcd(a, N) ≠ 1, return gcd.
2. Find period r of function f(x) = a^x mod N
   (i.e., smallest r s.t. a^r ≡ 1 mod N).
   ← Use quantum register and QFT to find r in poly time.
3. If r is odd or a^(r/2) ≡ -1 mod N: retry.
4. Compute gcd(a^(r/2) - 1, N) — likely non-trivial factor of N.

Step 2 (Period Finding):
    - Prepare quantum superposition |x⟩|0⟩ for x ∈ [0, q) where q ≈ N².
    - Apply f: |x⟩|0⟩ → |x⟩|a^x mod N⟩.
    - Measure second register; first register collapses to superposition of x with same a^x.
    - Apply QFT to first register; measure.
    - Extract r via continued fraction expansion.
```

**Quantum DLP** 類似 framework。對 ECDLP, period finding in elliptic curve group。

## Results
- **1994 publication 立刻引發 post-quantum 研究**。
- **NIST 2016 launched PQ Standardization Project**, culminating in FIPS 203/204/205 (2024)。
- **Industry hybrid deployment**: Cloudflare (2022), Google (2023), Apple iMessage PQ3 (2024)。
- **Quantum hardware progress**: 從 1994 純理論到 2024 IBM Heron R2 ~1000 logical qubits — 30 年 progress 但仍距 cryptographically relevant (20M qubits) 遠。

## Limitations / what they don't solve
- **僅 abelian hidden subgroup problem**: Shor 對 non-abelian group (e.g., dihedral) 不適用。Hash-based crypto / lattice-based crypto 不在 attack scope。
- **Symmetric crypto 不受 (much) 影響**: Grover 1996 給 quadratic speedup but not exponential → AES-256 等 still secure。
- **Engineering challenge huge**: fault-tolerant quantum computer 需要 ~10^6 physical qubits per logical (with error correction). 2025 state-of-art ~100 logical。
- **Quantum vs classical actual cost**: Gidney-Ekerå 2021 estimate 比 Shor 原 paper 樂觀但仍需 millions of qubits。

## How it informs our protocol design
- **G6 必 PQ hybrid**：所有 classical KEM (X25519) 都假設「Shor 可破」，hybrid Kyber 救援。
- **G6 timeline 規劃**：v1 hybrid (2026)，v3 pure PQ (2035+)。對應 quantum hardware estimated timeline。
- **G6 對稱層 ChaCha20-Poly1305**：256-bit key, Grover 後仍 128-bit quantum security → 不需 immediate replacement。
- **G6 hash SHA-256**：256-bit output, BHT quantum collision 2^85 — acceptable for transcript hash (不需 simultaneous attack on millions of sessions)。

## Open questions
- **Fault-tolerant quantum hardware timeline**: 2030? 2040? 2050+? 仍 disputed。
- **Quantum hidden subgroup beyond abelian**: dihedral group hidden subgroup (relevant to lattice crypto) 仍 open quantum complexity-wise。
- **Quantum advantage on Grover-like sampling**: practical wins beyond Shor 在哪？
